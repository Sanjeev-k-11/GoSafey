Mithila Pilot Roadmap

Initial regional research, early infrastructure planning, and development of core platform features. This phase focuses on understanding local mobility needs and preparing for pilot operations.

Phase 2: Pilot Operations (Upcoming)

Gradual public rollout in Madhubani, followed by expansion across nearby regions based on pilot learnings.
