At GoSafey Ride, we aim for total transparency. As we build our mobility network in Bihar, our refund and cancellation policies are designed to be fair to both our riders and our hardworking Captains.

Policy 01

Cancellation Rules

Users may cancel a booking before the service begins. For Bike and Car rides, if a cancellation occurs after the Captain has arrived at the pickup location, a nominal cancellation fee may be applicable to compensate the partner for their time and fuel.

For Logistics services, cancellation timelines may vary based on service type, distance, and partner availability.
